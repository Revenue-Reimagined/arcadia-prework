A 60-Second Orientation

The GTM Gap has four stages.

One of these will feel familiar. Keep it in mind.

Stage 1

Stabilization

Every week is a new fire. Pipeline feels like fiction. Leadership can't agree on what the real problem is.

Stage 2

Foundation

There's traction, but it lives in a few people's heads. New hires take forever to ramp. The tech stack is bloated and underused.

Stage 3

Repeatability

Pockets of excellence that don't transfer. Top performers carry the team. Playbooks exist but nobody uses them.

Stage 4

Scalability

Growth is happening but feels fragile. What got you here is starting to crack. Expansion exposes gaps that were invisible at smaller scale.
